About Us

In 2022 we at Click, Click, Creatives, set out to create a resource website aimed towards providing new photographers an outlet to gain knowlege, information and confidence towards their photography skills. Our website contains featured creative photographers that provide insight towards their process, equiptment, and even give advice to creatives. We hope you gain some helpful advice and continue your creative journey with us!

Our Goals

Our primary goal here at Click, Click, Creatives is to simply make photography more accessible. We know how intimitating photography can be and wanted to give young individuals a platform to learn and expand their creativity within the amazing world that is photography. In addition, we wanted to build a community of like-minded creatives and share resources amongst creatives.
